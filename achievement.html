"Judi Online"

2023, Directed By Hilmi Abdrurrahim S

tells the story of a teenager who fell into online gambling
because he saw an advertisement on social media,
he did whatever it took to satisfy his addiction.

  • Director - Hilmi Abdrurrahim S
  • Story - Raddin Pratama R
  • Actor - Alif Alghifari
  • Best Cinematography, Editing - Harits Nur Fardansyah

Wining second place in short video competition by
Universitas Islam Indonesia.
